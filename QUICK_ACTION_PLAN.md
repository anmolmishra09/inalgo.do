## 💰 Pricing Calculator

### How to Quote Any Project:

**Step 1: Estimate Hours**
```
Small project (Landing page): 15-20 hours
Medium project (Business site): 30-40 hours
Large project (Web app): 60-80 hours
```

**Step 2: Calculate Base Price**
```
Your "student rate": $50/hour
Small project: 20 hours × $50 = $1,000
Medium project: 35 hours × $50 = $1,750
Large project: 70 hours × $50 = $3,500
```

**Step 3: Add 50% "Brand Premium"**
```
You're Inalgo (a brand), not "just a student"
Small: $1,000 × 1.5 = $1,500
Medium: $1,750 × 1.5 = $2,625 (~$2,500)
Large: $3,500 × 1.5 = $5,250 (~$5,000)
```

**Step 4: Adjust for Client Type**
```
Local business struggling: -20%
Startup with funding: +20%
Agency white-label: Hourly ($40-45/hr)
```

## ⚠️ RED FLAGS to Avoid

### Bad Clients (Say NO to these):
- ❌ "I have an idea worth millions, I just need a developer"
- ❌ "Can you build Facebook/Instagram clone for $500?"
- ❌ "I'll pay you after it makes money"
- ❌ "This should only take a few hours, right?"
- ❌ Won't sign a contract
- ❌ Wants "just one more thing" every day

### Good Clients (Say YES to these):
- ✅ Has a clear problem to solve
- ✅ Reasonable timeline expectations
- ✅ Willing to pay 50% upfront
- ✅ Responsive to communication
- ✅ Values your expertise
- ✅ Has budget for the project
